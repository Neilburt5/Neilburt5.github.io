Who I am

About Me

I'm a Mechatronics Engineering student at SDU Syddansk Universitet in Denmark, graduating in 2026. My work sits at the intersection of control engineering, robotics, and machine learning — I enjoy the full stack of a system, from modelling the physics to deploying the code.

I've designed PID flight controllers for autonomous drones, fine-tuned large language models for embedded systems (thesis graded 10/10), and currently lead the control and programming team at SDU's Micromouse Robotics Club. I'm drawn to problems that are messy in the physical world and require both rigorous engineering and creative thinking.

I've studied and worked internationally — an exchange semester in Shanghai and professional experience in Spain — and I thrive in multicultural, fast-moving environments.
